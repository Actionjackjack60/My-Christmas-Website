üí° Tips for Parents

Play Together: Join your kids for a round! Take turns or compete for the highest score. It's a great way to bond during the holidays.
Set Time Limits: Each game is short (60 seconds), making it easy to say "one more game" or "three more rounds." Use this to manage screen time effectively.
Celebrate Improvement: Instead of focusing on winning, celebrate when kids beat their personal best scores. This encourages persistence and growth mindset.
Make it Educational: Memory Match is great for practicing memory skills. Ask your child about their strategy for remembering card locations!
Device Tips: The games work best with a mouse or trackpad. On tablets, make sure the screen is clean for accurate touch controls.
